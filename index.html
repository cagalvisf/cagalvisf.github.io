About

I am a physicist initially specialized in gravitation before expanding my focus to the field of quantum mechanics, which led me to complete a master’s degree in quantum computing. This academic journey has brought me to Finland, where I am currently pursuing a PhD at Aalto University. Nowadays I work in the intersection of quantum computing and machine learning. At Aalto University, I am a doctoral researcher under the supervision of Professor Simo Särkkä. I am part of the QuantLearn project, an ambitious research initiative funded by the Research Council of Finland. The project aims to advance the frontiers of knowledge by developing quantum machine learning algorithms with the potential to transform various domains within computational science.
